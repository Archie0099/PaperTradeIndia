Bot Tournament

❓ How this works (click to read)
What you're seeing: a set of trading "bots", each with ₹1 crore of virtual money against real prices. They never place a real order. Some trade a single index; the BASKET bots dynamically pick & rebalance a portfolio of the best companies from a broad stock universe (the “active” hunters rotate weekly) — click any bot to see its full trade history: what it bought, when, and each trade's profit/loss.
Live % vs Track %: Live is the bot's latest trading day's return (today's move). Track is its total return over its whole life so far — the big picture.
Machine learning: three basket bots rank stocks with a local machine-learning model (a ridge regressor, a logistic classifier and a gradient-boosted tree ensemble) trained walk-forward on the price history — 100% free and offline, no external keys or API calls.
Evolve ⚙: a free, local genetic algorithm breeds new strategies by tweaking the current ones — across stocks, baskets and ML settings — backtests them, and adds any that beat the weakest bot to the board. No bot is ever retired — old and new strategies stay so you can compare them side by side (the board grows up to a cap). Bots tagged ·g3 were born in generation 3.
F&O bots sell options (straddles/strangles/condors); their option prices are modelled with Black-Scholes (no free option history exists), so treat F&O numbers as indicative. Click any bot to highlight its line and read what it does in plain English.
💡 Click any bot to open its full portfolio, strategy rationale & trade history →
Equity race — growth since each bot's track start, on a log scale (so a 10× and a 100× bot are both visible across the ~20-year history). Lines are coloured by strategy kind; the highlighted line is the last bot you opened. Marked to market: the curves include the unrealised profit/loss of any open positions, so a line can move on a day with no trade.

Auto-Pilot

❓ How Auto-Pilot works (click to read)
Auto-Pilot picks the best bot from the tournament (in Auto mode it follows the best risk-adjusted (Sharpe) bot — its no-hindsight walk-forward pick — or switch to Manual and pick a bot yourself) and copies its portfolio onto your own ₹1-crore account. It places the same trades through the normal engine, so your Positions, P&L and Order History all update — and you can watch a bot trade for you. It only trades when the bot rebalances (no churn on quiet days).
It manages your whole account: when active it will buy/sell to match the bot, including replacing any positions you opened manually. Use “Start fresh ₹1 cr” for a clean slate. Pause stops new trades and leaves your positions as they are.
F&O option legs are copied and then re-priced live from the underlying with the same indicative Black-Scholes model the bot uses (there's no free live option feed), so your copy tracks the bot's P&L between rebalances. Everything is virtual money — no real order is ever sent.
